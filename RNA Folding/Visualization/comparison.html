Image 1

Both these images represent the secondary structure of RNA sequence - CAUUAUUACUUUUGGUACGCGCUGUGACACUUCAAACUCGUACCGUGAGUAAUAAUGCG. The left image is the one generated by RNACentral, a reputed website to gather information about biological molecular structures. Whereas, the image on the right is generated by our own Dynamic Programming based algorithm. Both of these figures display a total of 21 base pairs, hence validating that our code works as intended.
